Mobile Game

Search & Destroy

Description

Search&Destroy is an action game set in the Golden Age of military aviation dedicated to aviation from the early 20th century to the most advanced modern combat units. Throwing players into a never-ending battle for dominance of the skies.

Steer the plane and follow the dotted line to ensure that your rocket is aimed at the enemy aircraft. Do you think it's so simple? In our game it is easy to learn to fly, but it is difficult to win. You will be forced to avoid hostile missiles, turbulence zones, static fields and many other things. Collect stars and use them to buy new planes & upgrades for your aircraft. Expand the performance of your airplane by upgrading max speed, acceleration, turning and flares parameters. Collect Power-ups like Shield, Boost, FOV to help destroy and evade missiles.

Features：
✔Free to Play
✔Top quality 2D graphics.
✔Modern aircraft, jet and warplane battle in 2D.
✔Smooth and simple aircraft control system.
✔Easy to learn hard to master.
✔Intuitive controls optimized for mobile devices.
✔Runs very smooth at 60fps on average devices.
